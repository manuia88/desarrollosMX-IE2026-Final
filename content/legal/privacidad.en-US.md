## 7. Your rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the following rights:

- **Right to know / access:** request the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected
- **Right to delete:** request deletion of personal information we have collected, subject to legal exceptions
- **Right to correct:** request correction of inaccurate personal information
- **Right to opt out of sale/sharing:** opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (as defined under CCPA/CPRA)
- **Right to limit use of sensitive personal information:** limit the use and disclosure of sensitive categories
- **Right to data portability:** receive your personal information in a portable format
- **Right to non-discrimination:** not be discriminated against for exercising your rights

### 7.1 How to exercise your rights

Send a request to privacy@desarrollosmx.com including:

1. Full name and registered email
2. Verification information sufficient to confirm your identity
3. Description of the right you wish to exercise
4. Details facilitating the location of your information

We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable state law (generally 45 days under CCPA/CPRA, extendable by an additional 45 days with notice).

### 7.2 Authorized agents

You may designate an authorized agent to submit requests on your behalf, subject to verification requirements.

## 8. Data retention

We retain personal information for the time necessary to fulfill the purposes and applicable legal retention periods:

- **Active accounts:** while the account remains active
- **Cancelled accounts:** up to 24 months after cancellation
- **Security logs:** up to 12 months
- **Data with specific legal retention:** as required by applicable U.S. law

## 9. Security measures

We implement reasonable technical, administrative, and physical safeguards:

- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- Role-based access control and multi-factor authentication
- Audit logs
- Incident response policies
- Staff training on privacy and security

No measure is infallible. In the event of a security incident materially affecting your rights, we will notify you as required by applicable state breach notification laws.

## 10. Cookies and similar technologies

We use first- and third-party cookies for authentication, preferences, aggregate analytics, and fraud prevention. You may configure your browser to reject cookies, with potential impact on functionality. Where applicable state law requires, we honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals as opt-out preference signals.

## 11. Minors

The Platform is not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information.
